Mission


Our mission is to connect Non-for-Profit organizations and donors. We hope to allow Non-for-Profit organizations to search for corporations and grants to partner with. Also, guiding individuals to volunteer opportunities and give physical and monetary donations to organizations that align with their values.

The founders of Philantroportal, found ourselves drawn together by a shared desire to utilize our technical skills for social good.
